# Product Guidelines

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Modern Corporate:** The portfolio will employ a clean, sophisticated design characterized by ample whitespace, a professional color palette (e.g., deep blues, slate grays, and crisp whites), and high-quality typography to convey trust and professional excellence.

## Content & Copywriting
- **Impact-Focused:** Experience and project descriptions will use strong action verbs to highlight results and achievements.
- **Balanced Narrative:** Each project will include a brief narrative of the challenge and solution, complemented by technical specifications and data-backed outcomes.
- **Clarity and Precision:** Messaging will be concise, technical where appropriate, and formatted for easy scanning.

## User Interface & Design Principles
- **Responsive & Mobile-First:** The application must provide a seamless and high-quality experience across all devices and screen sizes.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Information architecture will be logical, with clear labeling to minimize user effort in finding key details.
- **Sophisticated Interactions:** Interactive elements will feature subtle transitions and smooth animations, balancing a professional feel with modern technical precision.

## Functional Guidelines
- **Integrated Resume Viewer:** The professional resume will be accessible via a high-quality integrated viewer directly within the site, ensuring recruiters can view details without leaving the platform.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure the site adheres to modern accessibility standards (WCAG) to be inclusive to all users.
